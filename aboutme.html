About Me

Hello! I'm Karuna Nikam, an enthusiastic Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning student at "D. Y. Patil College of Engineering and Technology" in Kolhapur. I have a deep passion for exploring the possibilities of AI and ML and turning them into practical solutions.

Throughout my academic journey, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects that have allowed me to apply my knowledge and skills to real-world scenarios. Some of my notable achievements include:

  • Virtual Assistant: I developed a virtual assistant that streamlines everyday tasks, making life more convenient. This project involved natural language processing (NLP) techniques and demonstrated my ability to create intelligent systems that respond to user commands.
  • Chatbot for College Admission: I designed a chatbot to simplify the college admission process. Using NLP, the chatbot provides prospective students with information and guidance, making their application process smooth and efficient.
  • Dropshipping Recommendation System: Leveraging machine learning, I created a recommendation system for dropshipping businesses. This system helps online retailers make data-driven decisions by suggesting the best products to offer based on market trends and customer preferences.
  • Dress Code Detection System: I delved into the world of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to develop a dress code detection system. This innovative project identifies and classifies clothing styles, aiding in applications like fashion e-commerce and event management.
